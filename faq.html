Frequently Asked Questions

Getting Started

What is Campus Connect?
Campus Connect is a platform that connects students, faculty, and staff across our university, providing a space to share ideas, collaborate, and build relationships.
How do I sign up for Campus Connect?
To sign up, simply click on the "Register" button at the top right corner of our website and follow the registration process using your university email address.

Account and Profile

How do I edit my profile?
To edit your profile, click on your profile picture at the top right corner of the website, then click on "Edit Profile" and make the necessary changes.
How do I change my password?
To change your password, click on your profile picture at the top right corner of the website, then click on "Settings" and follow the instructions to update your password.

Groups and Communities

How do I join a group?
To join a group, click on the "Groups" tab and browse through the available groups. Click on the group you're interested in and click the "Join" button.
How do I create a new group?
To create a new group, click on the "Groups" tab and click the "Create Group" button. Fill out the required information and submit your request.

Events and Activities

How do I find events on Campus Connect?
To find events, click on the "Events" tab and browse through the upcoming events. You can also use the search bar to find specific events.
How do I RSVP to an event?
To RSVP to an event, click on the event you're interested in and click the "RSVP" button. Follow the instructions to confirm your attendance.

Technical Issues

I'm having trouble logging in. What do I do?
If you're having trouble logging in, try resetting your password or contact our support team at info@campusconnect.com for assistance.
The website is not loading properly. What do I do?
Try clearing your browser cache and cookies, or contact our support team at info@campusconnect.comfor assistance.
